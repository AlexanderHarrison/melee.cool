Hi, I'm Aitch!

I'm a programmer. I create tools and mods for the game Super Smash Bros. Melee. I'm the author of rwing and the organizer and main contributer to Training Mode - Community Edition.

I also work on a lot of smaller tools. I wrote a text editor, edit, which I daily drive. I wrote a graphics programming wrapper, ezcompute, which makes writing small graphics visualizations and programs much simpler. I have a ton of small melee tools: a slp replay parser, a compressed slp format, an iso filesystem editor, an TM recording gci file creator, a combo finder, a dat file editor, and a melee assets extractor,

I'm a melee player. I'm active in the Edmonton and larger Alberta scenes. I main Peach, because I enjoy pressing buttons, labbing and practicing niche tech, and being able to take breaks without getting demolished because I misinputted. I should make the 2025 Alberta PR (mostly because half the 2024 PR quit). I don't have a particular favourite matchups, but I strongly dislike fighting campy sheiks, puffs, and spacies.

Most of my time is spent working on rwing and TM-CE these days. If you'd like to support my work, you can join my patreon. My patrons keep a roof over my head. I am incredibly grateful to every one of them. Thank you all so much.

If you would like to reach out to me, you can ping me in my discord. Most conversation there is suggesting ideas or reporting bugs for my projects, but you can feel free to ask me anything.
