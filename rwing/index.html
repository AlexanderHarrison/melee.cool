rwing

rwing is an advanced replay viewer for Super Smash Bros. Melee.

With rwing, you can easily browse and watch back your SLP replay files. Review tournament vods for mistakes. Study top-level gameplay vods. Export missed combos as UnclePunch Training Mode savestates. Share annoted vods with students.

Available for windows, linux, and macos!

Download rwing on my patreon

Features

  • Search and filter through thousands of slp files instantly
  • Export UnclePunch's training mode savestates
  • Create notes and diagrams that save into the replay
  • Explore a featureful game timeline
  • Compress your slp files to 1/10th the size
  • View hitboxes
  • Find missed wavedashes, actionable moments, and more
  • Quickly and easily find combos
  • Browse, delete, and rename your TM savestates
  • View and modify Clippi playlists
  • Take screenshots of the game with your diagrams
  • Change panel layout to show info where you need it
  • Customize theming and hotkeys
  • And more!

Overview

Filtering Replays

In rwing you can filter thousands of slp files instantly. Filter by tag, slippi connect code, character, stage, and singles/teams.

You can also mark special games with a star to find them easier, and filter for games that you have annotated.

UnclePunch's Training Mode Exports

You can export any moment in your games as a Training Mode savestate. This makes practicing your mistakes simple. You can start to fix any issue with your play immediately.

Then, you can open the savestate in Training Mode to practice.
